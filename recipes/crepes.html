Crepes

Description

These crepes are based on authentic street crepes found in Paris.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Add flour and salt to a bowl
  2. Mix flour mixture to remove clumps
  3. Add milk and eggs to a separate bowl
  4. Pour a half cup of batter onto a hot crepe pan
  5. Flip crepes
  6. Plate crepe and add more batter to pan
